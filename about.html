Welcome to

Value In HR!

We focus on providing technical expertise across all aspects of Human Resource Management and Human Resource Development and work in partnership with our clients to help unlock the value of Human Capital. Since managing human resources efficiently and intelligently is critical to our company's success, it is vital to find a service provider who can offer the right mix of services. Whether you want a bundled package or prefer to pick and choose the services you need, we will review the options available to you. The marketplace is crowded with companies to choose from, but we have researched some of the best providers for the small-to-medium sized customer.

  • Your HR team's entire focus is on administrative work. When your staff spends most of their time on data input and forms processing, this may be a signal it is time to look at outsourcing or upgrading to more agile HR systems. Limiting your team to largely administrative work takes members focus off strategic contributions.
  • Employees lack access to self-service Small business management no longer need to conduct every aspect of benefits enrollment, updating employee information, retirement plan information, or employee training. Leading HR technologies provide employees with  self-service portals that help them complete many basic tasks themselves, such as updating their mailing address or adjusting retirement plan contribution amounts. Our HR team can free up management’s time, and many employees find the process more convenient, since they will be having access the portal anytime and anywhere.
  • HR is becoming a cost center. Ideally, your HR team should function as a key strategic asset, not a liability. It may be time to ask yourself whether external expertise or better technology can help.
